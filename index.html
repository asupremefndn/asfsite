About Us

A-Supreme Foundation began as a compassionate response to the plight of seniors who are unable to afford in-home care and as a result were at risk of being adversely compromised in their health and quality of life. Assigned to one such request, a Personal Support Worker (PSW) on arrival at the senior’s home called the A-Supreme Nursing and Home Care (ASNHC) office to assure the founders that even if she was not paid for the job, she would attend to the needs of the senior because of the deplorable state in which she found them. Inundated with similar requests, the founders of our parent corporation (ASNHC) Agency established the A-Supreme Foundation to fill the gap and help these seniors live with dignity. Since its founding in 2014, the Foundation has focused its attention on providing advocacy, education and health support services to seniors across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Mission

A-Supreme Foundation is dedicated to improving the quality of life for seniors, through education, advocacy, and resources

Vision

Be a global leader in advocating for the best quality of life for seniors.

Values

Empathy. Integrity. Empowerment. Innovation. Respect. Dignity.

Mandate

In December 2014, A-Supreme Foundation became a registered Canadian Charity to strengthen its capacity to respond to the needs of those most vulnerable. The foundation’s overarching focus is to improve the quality of life for vulnerable seniors in the GTA.

Funding

ASF programs are funded by private and corporate donations, fundraising activities and some grant funding from the government.

Outlook

It is our intent over time to extend our programs and services beyond the GTA to across Canada and play our part in ensuring that no senior is denied the care and dignity they deserve.

At A-Supreme Foundation, we envision a world in which all of the elderly and infirm unable to care for themselves will be cared for by their communities. Make an active difference by donating or volunteering with us.
