About Us

A-Supreme Foundation began as a compassionate response to the plight of seniors who are unable to afford in-home care and as a result were at risk of being adversely compromised in their health and quality of life. Since its founding in 2014, the Foundation has focused its attention on providing advocacy, education and health support services to seniors across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA).

Mission

We are dedicated to improving the quality of life for seniors, through education, advocacy, and resources.

Vision

Be a global leader in advocating for the best quality of life for seniors.

Values

Empathy. Integrity. Empowerment. Innovation. Respect. Dignity.
